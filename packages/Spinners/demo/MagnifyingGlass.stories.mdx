## ✨ MagnifyingGlass: Mesmerizing Magnifying Glass! ✨

Introducing the *MagnifyingGlass* component, a captivating addition to your UI that brings a mesmerizing magnifying glass to life. 🧐💫

Whether you're looking to add a touch of elegance to your design or want to create a dynamic loading animation, the MagnifyingGlass component is here to infuse life into your projects. 🎨🔄

### Features:

- **Dynamic Animation**: MagnifyingGlass gracefully animates a magnifying glass, creating an elegant and engaging visual experience. 🌟🎥
- **Customization**: You can customize the color, width, height, and visibility of the MagnifyingGlass component to suit your design needs. 🎨📏
- **Accessibility**: MagnifyingGlass includes accessibility attributes to ensure a seamless user experience. ♿🌐

```jsx
